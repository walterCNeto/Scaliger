As epactas que colheste, subtrai de 36 dias, 23 horas, 1080 escrúpulos. O que restar serão os dias a partir das Calendas de Setembro. Este método, em meu juízo, é melhor que o outro, por ser mais expedito. Resta a doutrina das Tekuphoth, que nos é fácil conhecer, a nós que usamos a forma juliana: bastando que saibamos estabelecer a sede da neomênia de Tisri no mês juliano. Pois a Tekuphá de Tisri é sempre em 24 de Setembro. Mas, para que nada falte à doutrina do Cômputo Judaico, também devemos tratar disso. O que será mais fácil a partir destas duas Tábuas do que a partir de outros Cômputos Judaicos. REMAINDER OF THE TABLE OF NEW MOONS OF TISRI IN YEARS EXTENDED BY SIX CYCLES.

it will never deceive you, provided you have proceeded properly and in the prescribed order. But the other method which we shall present is no worse. Take the epacts you have collected and subtract them from 36 days, 23 hours, 1080 parts. What remains will be the days from the Kalends of September. Therefore subtract the epacts you have collected—namely 32, 13, 380—from 36, 23, 1080. There remain 4 days, 10 hours, 700 parts. Thus the new moon of Tisri in the year 5355 falls after the 4th day from the Kalends of September: and since it is the fifth weekday, it will accordingly be on September 5. This method, in my judgment, is better than the other, because it is more expeditious. There remains the doctrine of the Tekuphoth, which is easy for us to know, since we use the Julian form: provided we know how to establish the seat of the neomenia of Tisri in the Julian month. For the Tekupha of Tisri is always on September 24. But, so that nothing of the doctrine of the Jewish Computus be lacking, this too must be treated by us. Which will be easier from these two Tables than from other Jewish Computi. From 5254 years and the epacts of the third year, combined as we have already seen, there result 31 days, 37 hours, 380 parts. From these always subtract 12 days, 20 hours, 204 parts. There will remain 19 days, 17 hours, 176 parts. To these add the hours and parts of the new moon of Tisri. They amount to 19 days, 21 [hours]. Therefore the Tekupha of Tisri in the year 5355 falls on day 20, hour 21 from the beginning of the night, that is, hour 9 of the day. RESIDUUM TABULAE NOVILUNIORUM TISRI IN ANNIS EXPANSIS PER SENOS CYCLOS.

[Tabulae: vide sectionem tables]

nunquam tibi imponet, modo rite, & ordine praescripto feceris. Sed non deterior est altera, quam afferemus. Epactas, quas collegisti, aufer a diebus 36, 23, 1080. Reliqui erunt dies a Kalendis Septembris. Ergo epactas, quas collegisti, nempe 32, 13, 380, aufer a 36, 23, 1080. Remanent dies 4, 10, 700. Itaque novilunium Tisri anni 5355 accidit post 4 diem a Kal. Septembris: & quia est feria quinta, erit ergo in v. Septembris. Haec methodus meo iudicio melior est altera: quia expeditior. Superest doctrina Tekupharum, quam nobis promptum est scire, qui Iuliana forma utimur: modo neomeniae Tisri sedem in mense Iuliano statuere sciamus. Semper enim Tekupha Tisri est 24 Septembris. Sed ne quid doctrinae Computi Iudaici desit, id quoque nobis tractandum. Quod facilius erit ex his duabus Tabulis, quam ex aliis Computis Iudaicis. Annorum 5254, & III. epactae simul compositae, ut iam vidimus, conflant dies 31, 37, 380. ex illis semper abiice dies 12, 20, 204. supererunt dies 19, horae 17, 176. quibus adiice horas & scrupula novilunii Tisri. Confiunt dies 19, 21. Igitur Tekupha Tisri anni 5355 incidit in diem 20, hora 21, ab initio noctis, id est, hora 9 diei. Nunc de translationibus feriarum in neomenia Tisri dicendum.
