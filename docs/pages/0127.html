English

in the reckoning of Attic time. We have also added [πρώτας ἡμέρας] "first days": A, which always differ, as we said, by one unit from the Olympic ones in the fourth year, on account of the Julian bissextile and the Olympic bissextile. Now let us test its reliability. Let the example just adduced by us be taken up again. The 401st year of Iphitus, with 76 cast off, as far as is possible, is the 21st of the sixth Olympic period. If, when a whole period has been adjusted to these (as must be done whenever the number that is subtracted is greater than one)—

REMAINDER OF THE TABLE OF ATTIC NEOMENIAE IN JULIAN MONTHS.

Notas do tradutor: Página contém parte final do texto do capítulo e a continuação (residuum) da Tábua das Neomênias Áticas em meses julianos, cobrindo os anos 39–76 do período. Os cabeçalhos em grego da tabela são os nomes dos doze (mais um, o embolímico Ποσειδεών β) meses áticos. A última coluna fornece um valor numérico residual (epacta ou contagem de dias), essencial à função da tábua.
