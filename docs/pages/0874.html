English

Index page (Index of subjects and authors) for letters H and I. Lists authors and topics with page references and fragments. Notable entries include: Haggai the Prophet, Haie the Spanish Rabbi, Haiton the Armenian, Harpocration, Hecataeus of Abdera, Hermannus Contractus, Herodotus, Hesiod, Hesychius, Jerome (with his supplement to Eusebius's Chronicle), Hilarion the monk of Verona, Hildericus the Gaul, Hipparchus, Hippolytus the Bishop, the Indian History, the Barbaro-Greek Patriarchal History of Martin Crusius, Homer, Horace, the Horologium or Menologium of the Church of Constantinople, Horapollo. For letter I: Jacob son of Asher in Via Vitae (with error), Jeremiah the Prophet (with Lamentations), Jesus Sirach, Ignatius Patriarch of Antioch, the Little Iliad, Index of the deeds of Augustus, Pope Innocent, St. John the Evangelist, Johannes Cuspinianus, Johannes of Saxony, Johannes Leo Africanus (with errors), Johannes Leunclavius (with errors in comparing the years of the Hegira with the Dionysian Christian era throughout the entire context of the Turkish annals), Johannes Lucidus, the Targum of Jonathan, error of Iose the Hebrew doctor, Josephus (with extensive list of pages, errors, and emendations, including the time when he was writing), errors of the copyists in Josephus, the book of Joshua, the binding of Isaac, Isaacius Argyrus (with error), Isaiah the Prophet, Isidore the Cardinal, Isidore Bishop of Seville, the book of Judges, Judith, Julian the Apostate [ὁ παραβάτης], Julian Pomerius Bishop of Toledo, Julius Capitolinus, Julius Firmicus (with error). Catchword at foot: Vvv 3 Iustinus.
