English

Index page (index of authors and works cited), letters P (end), R, S, and beginning of T. Lists ancient and medieval authors with the page numbers where they appear in Scaliger's work, often with notes such as 'emendatur' (is emended), 'notatur' (is annotated), 'error' (error), 'fragm.' or 'fr.' (fragment/fragmentary section). Notable entries include: Procopius, Propertius, Prosper of Aquitaine, Prudentius, Ptolemy (with numerous references and emendations), the parapegma attributed to Ptolemy, the Decree of the Athenians in favor of Hyrcanus, Ethnarch of the Jews, and in favor of Zeno, the Decree of Timocrates; the Book of Kings, Remigius, Rhenanus, Rufinus interpreter of Josephus, Rufus Festus V.C., Rutilius Namatianus the poet; Saadia Gaon Hebrew doctor, Landulphus Sagax, R. Solomon Hebrew doctor, the Book of Samuel, Sanchuniathon of Berytus, the Sanhedrin (or house of judgment), the Saracenica of Sylburgius (with many errors), I. of Saxony, Lambert of Schafnaburg, [ὁ χοινομέτης τῆς οὐσίας — common measurer of substance?], scholiast of Aristophanes (emended/error), scholiast of Germanicus Caesar, scholiast of Pindar; M. Scotus (see Marianus), Seneca, Seneca the Tragedian, Sepúlveda the Spaniard (annotated), Servius (emended), Servius of F. Modius, Severus Sulpitius Nitiobrix, Sibyl, Sibylline oracles (emended), Sidonius Apollinaris (emended), Sigebert monk of Gembloux (error, remarkable hallucination), Simplicius the philosopher, Sisenna, Socrates the ecclesiastical historian, Solinus, Proverbs of Solomon, Sophonias the prophet, Sophronius, Sozomen, Spartianus, Statius Papinius (emended), the Greek Stele of Ancona, an ancient and remarkable monument (illustrated), Stephanus of Byzantium (de Urbibus), Stobaeus's Physica (emended), Strabo, Suetonius (annotated, error), Suidas (emended), the Syriac Paraphrast of the New Testament; Tabula παρανατελλόντων (Table of co-rising stars), Tabularies of the Phoenicians, Cor. Ta- [catchword].
