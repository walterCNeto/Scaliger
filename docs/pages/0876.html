English

Index of authors and works cited, letters N, O, P (partial). Each entry gives a proper name followed by page and folio references (with a/b/c/d suffixes indicating page quadrant) where the author is cited, examined, corrected, or where an error is pointed out. The abbreviations 'pr.' (prolegomena/proemium), 'fr.' (fragmentum), 'fragmen.', and 'error' indicate the type of citation. Notable entries: Nehemiah (notable passage at 648.a); Pindar, notable passage on the time of the Olympic games; Plutarch, with numerous corrections and errors noted; Πέτρου Ἀποστόλου περιόδων ('Periodoi of the Apostle Peter'), author cited at 6.pr.38; Φοινίκων ἀναγραφαί ('Annals of the Phoenicians'). The page ends with the gathering signature 'Vvv 4' and the beginning of the next entry 'G. Po-' (G. Postellus, presumably).

Flags de incerteza (pontos para revisão humana)
  • Entrada 'mij fragmen. 24.25.50' no topo: o nome (terminado em '-mij') está cortado da página anterior; provavelmente 'Hieronymij' ou 'Maimonidis' truncado.
  • Pindarus: 'locus notabilis de tempore ludicri Olympici 2.pr.36.25.a.52.d' — número 36 confirmado no scan, mas a sequência '2.pr.36.25.a.52.d' é incomum.
  • Πέτρου Ἀποστόλου περιόδων: a forma 'Πέτρϛ Ἀπόστολϛ' no original usa o sigma final ϛ, possivelmente representando -ου (genitivo); transcrição preserva grafia do original.
Notas do tradutor: Página de índice (frontmatter posterior) — letras N, O, P. Sem conteúdo astronômico direto, embora cite autores cronológica e astronomicamente importantes (Phlegon, Philochorus, Plutarchus, Porphyrius). A assinatura 'Vvv 4' indica caderno Vvv, fólio 4.
