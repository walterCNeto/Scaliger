Tables from Book IV of the Emendation of Times. See the 'tables' section for values. Headings: Magnitude of the solar year of Rabbi Adda / Difference; Difference of the enneadecaeterides of Rabbi Adda and the sidereal ones; Difference between the sidereal year and the year of Rabbi Adda; Precession of the equinox [ΠΡΟΗΓΗΣΙΣ ΙΣΗμερινή] in the year of Rabbi Adda.
