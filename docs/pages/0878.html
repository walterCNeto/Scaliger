English

Index page (continuation) of the Opus de Emendatione Temporum, covering letters T, V, X, Z. It lists authors, works and sources cited, with page and section references (a, b, c, d) and fragments (fr.). Among the authors listed are: Cornelius Tacitus, the Targum of Jonathan, Tatian, Terence, Tertullian, Theodoret, Theocritus, Theognis, Theon the Mathematician, Theon the scholiast of Aratus, Theophylact, Theophrastus, Thucydides, Tibullus, Timocharis, Trogus, Tzetzes; Varro, Ludovicus Vartomannus, Velleius Paterculus, ancient inscriptions and monuments, Victor of Capua, Victorinus (founder of the Paschal cycles), the letters of Vigilius bishop of Trent and of Pope Vigilius, Vincent of Beauvais, Virgil (misunderstood / explained), Vitruvius, Ulpian the Rhetorician, Ulpian the Jurist, Volusius Maecianus; Xenophon, Xiphilinus; the Prophet Zachariah, Zonaras, Zosimus. The word "INDEX" appears at the lower right as a catchword.
