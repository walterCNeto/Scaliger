The whole page is a chronological-astronomical table in two halves (left and right), each with columns: Anni Collecti (collected years), Character Anni [Feria, Hours, First scruple], Προήγησις χρονική (chronological precession) [Days, Hours, First scruple], and Προήγησις τοπική (local/spatial precession) [Degrees, Scruple I, Scruple II].
