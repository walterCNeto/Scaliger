NOMENCLATOR OF THE AUTHORS AND WRITERS whose authority has been used in these books ON THE EMENDATION OF TIMES.

In the first prolegomena (pr. i.), the first number indicates the page, the second the line. In the Work on Emendation, the number indicates the page, and the letters a, b, c, d the place on the page. In the fragments (fr. i.), only the page is noted.
