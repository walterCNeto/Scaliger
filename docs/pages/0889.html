INDEX.

[This page is part of the work's analytical index. It consists of alphabetical entries in two columns, principally concerning 'Mauriacus locus', 'Maxentius', 'Medes', and 'Months' (of the various nations: Egyptians, Ethiopians, Antiochenes, Hagarene Arabs, Armenians, civil and celestial Athenians, Bithynians, Chaldeans, Coptic Christians, Danes, Gazenses, of the Hegira, Jews, Greek Macedonians, Syrian Macedonians, celestial Muslims, Mexicans, Persians, Peruvians, celestial of Ptolemy Philadelphus, ancient Romans, Samaritans, Saxons, Thebans, Tyrians); and in the right column a section 'MONTHS OF THE NATIONS ACCORDING TO THEIR SERIES' arranged alphabetically (Ab, Aban, Adar, Adarpahascht, Aegon, Aheli, Alalcomenius, Amschir, Anthesterion, Aormaanet, Apellaeus, Aphrodisius, Aprilis, Aratz, Arichi, Arius, Artemisius, Aschbat/Schebat, Asphandar, Athyr, Auca Ycuzqui Intiraymi, Audynaeus, Augustus, Ayamara, Behemen, Bendiaeus, Blothmonath, Bludemaanet, Boedromion, Bucatius, Buzi, Cagutz, Camay, Casleu, Caslim, Chadar, Chahua Huarqui, Chardad, Chiac, Choeac, Ghosturmonath, Christmaanet, Ciuailhuilt, Coauitleuac, Conum, Corayme, Daesius, Damatrius, December, Delevv, Demetrius, Di, Didymon, Dionysius, Dios, Dioscuros, Dulhagiathi, Dulkaadathi, Dystrus, Eçalcua-). Flags de incerteza (pontos para revisão humana)
  • 'Ghosturmonath VIII Saxonum' inicia com 'Gh' no scan, possivelmente erro tipográfico para 'Chosturmonath' (no esperado ordem alfabética estaria após 'Choeac')
  • 'Conum III & IV Syrorum' — leitura possível também como 'Conun' ou 'Comum'
  • 'Mexicanorum εἰκοσιημερῷ' — transliteração grega da palavra 'vigesimal/de vinte dias'; leitura do diacrítico não totalmente nítida
  • Última entrada cortada na base da página: 'Eçalcua-' continua na próxima
